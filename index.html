About Dan

Almost before he could walk, Dan was powering himself around on two wheels, after his Dad bought him 50cc quad and motocross bikes. He was racing around a 100cc quad at the age of five and started karting at seven.


At 15 Dan moved from karts into cars, competing in the Ginetta Junior Winter Series and took his first victory in the main championship the following year, making history by winning just his fifth race in the series.


Staying on the British Touring Car Championship support package, he had three years in the competitive Renault UK Clio Cup before moving to the Mini Challenge for 2019, in the JCW class. With two wins, nine podiums, three pole positions, and third place in the championship, Dan remains one to watch.

Biography
